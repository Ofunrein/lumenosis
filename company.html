Unorthodox was born from a profound passion to pioneer transformation in the business world through artificial intelligence. Recognizing the limitless potential of AI to automate tasks, streamline processes, and enable smarter decision-making, the Unorthodox team set out on their mission to revolutionize the way businesses operate.

Driven by a vision to break the norm and push the boundaries of what's possible, they dedicated themselves to create bespoke AI solutions that blend seamlessly into existing infrastructures. Their aim was not merely to automate processes, but to create intelligent systems that learn, adapt, and continually optimize operations.

About Unorthodox

Unorthodox was founded by Liam Evans & Mark Wilson, two entrepreneurs that have spent years of their life building businesses and working closely with business owners, seeing first hand the pain and effort that goes into running companies.

"AI is not just a technological trend, it's the future cornerstone of businesses worldwide. It is redefining traditional methods, enhancing operational efficiency, and facilitating unprecedented growth. Those who embrace it today will be the industry leaders of tomorrow." - Unorthodox

Their first-hand experience sparked a realization: there had to be a better, smarter way to manage the myriad of tasks that come with running a business. Believing in the transformative potential of artificial intelligence, they established Unorthodox. This venture merged their business acumen with a tech-forward approach, aiming to alleviate the pain points of businesses through intelligent automation.
